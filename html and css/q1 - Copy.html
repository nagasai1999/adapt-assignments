Virat Kohli-The Run Machine

Captain of India(Jersey no.18)

Virat Kohli(born 5th Nov,1988) is the current captain of Indian Cricket Team. He is the captain of Royal Challengers Bangalore in Indian Premier League. He is a right handed top order batsman. He is one of the very few players who is very consistent in ICC player rankings irrespective of the format. Kohli captained India U-19 cricket team which has won the 2008 world cup.
